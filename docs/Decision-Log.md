# Project Atlas Decision Log

## Decision 001

### Title

Rename the assistant from Atlas to RIO.

### Reason

Separate the project identity from the AI assistant.

Project Atlas refers to the complete platform.

RIO refers to the assistant users interact with.

### Impact

Improves clarity and allows multiple specialized agents such as Hermes and Athena.

## Decision 002

### Title

Adopt an object-oriented architecture for RIO.

### Reason

Represent the assistant as an object rather than a collection of functions.

### Impact

Future capabilities such as Hermes, Athena, memory, and AI integrations can be added without restructuring the application.
